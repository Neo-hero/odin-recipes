Oatmeal

Descritpion

healthly and delicous, this breakfast can for sure satisfy every household

Ingredients

  • milk
  • oats
  • blueberries
  • cinamon
  • honey

Directions

  1. pour 1/2 milk and oats
  2. stir until light smoke
  3. pour blueberries
  4. add cinamon
  5. drizzle with honey
  6. eat it
