Avocado Toast

Descritpion

Crunchy Toast with immense flavor, this dish can no doubt put smiles on peoples faces.

All you need is bread, avocado, and seasoning. And most importantly, Love

  • mushed avocado
  • Toast
  • Everything Bagel seasoning

Steps

  2. mush Avocado
  3. spread Avocado

  5. enjoy!!!
